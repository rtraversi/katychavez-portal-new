Calendar Integrations

Connect your calendar to view and create events from the portal.

Google Calendar
Checking…
Connected account: —

Clicking Connect will open Google's sign-in page. Choose your Google account and click Allow to grant calendar access. You'll be returned here automatically.

Only events on your primary calendar are shown. Access can be revoked at any time by clicking Disconnect or from myaccount.google.com/permissions.

Microsoft Outlook / Office 365
Checking…
Connected account: —

Clicking Connect will open Microsoft's sign-in page. Choose your Microsoft or Office 365 account and click Accept to grant calendar access. You'll be returned here automatically.

Only events on your primary calendar are shown. Access can be revoked at any time by clicking Disconnect or from myapps.microsoft.com.
